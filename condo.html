Our advantages

With The Frontenac project, we trade traditional timber construction for steel and concrete structures. After studying different types of concrete architecture, we chose the best available technology on the market. With areas ranging from 700 to 1500 square feet, these units remain in a more than acceptable price range, given the concrete construction refinement. You will also enjoy the following features: tremendous soundproofing, absence of timber vibration and greater security against fires, all that in a luxurious environment brightened by large size windows.
